# Mental-Health-EDA

From what we know, it can be seen how popular social media platforms such as TikTok and Instagram have different communication and media sharing capabilities. This can attract a population of audiences with similar interests and lead to a unique culture within certain platforms. Within these platforms is a large population of teen users who can become susceptible by the culture of a certain platform. This can lead to effects on mental health as expressed by factors such as sleep quality or academic performance. As a result, we want to look at data of teen mental health as expressed by certain lifestyle qualities to understand its relationship with their preferred social media platform. From this, we can gain an idea of whether a platform is likely to foster a negative or positive culture for its teen users.
